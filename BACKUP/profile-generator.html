— Static Generative Art —

Generative art that produces a still image that doesn't move, but is randomized every time it's generated


What is Generative Art? — An interactive article that gives an overview of what exactly generative art is, and explains key concepts behind it. (2022)
Generative Art Gallery — A diverse collection of generative artworks I've created. Every piece will look completely different every time you redraw it, allowing for infinite unique designs and artworks. (2022)
Connectivity — Generate grids filled with random blocks that all connect to make continuous shapes, selecting up to 20 different types of unique blocks. (2022)
Bands — Generative designs formed by colorful interlocking bands make interesting patterns resembling machinery or spirals. (2021)
Tiles — Generate a random colorful grid of up to 16 different types of tiles, with endless combinations and palettes. (2022)
Random Walker Shading — A plane is shaded and colored based on the movements of a random walker through thousands of steps, creating colorful and intricate designs resembling galaxies or clouds. (2021)

— Dynamic Generative Art —

Generative art that moves and changes over time, constantly shifting and updating


Generative Art Sketchpad — Draw abstract designs using different brushes that each have their own unique style and design. (2021)
Branching — Create lots of intricate patterns with colorful, branching trails. (2021)
Dots — Create spirograph-like patterns by changing how colored dots move around in ellipse paths. (2021)
Servo — Control how synchronized jointed arms rotate around each other, creating mesmerizing motions and designs. (2021)
Random Stripe Walker — A walker moves randomly across the canvas, drawing rectangular stripes in the direction it moves, forming interconnected paths and trails. (2022)
Living Art Gallery — An abstract, moving art gallery. (2021)
Metaballs — Watch as colorful, blobby metaballs float and squish around the screen in a lava lamp-like way. (2021)

— Natural Generative Art —

Generative art that replicates and stylizes different processes that can be found in nature


Brownian Tree — Generate and customize colorful tree-like growths made out of dots generated by Brownian motion, which mimics the natural phenomenon of Diffusion-Limited Aggregation. (2021)
Brownian Snowflake — Generate icy, colorful snowflakes using symmetrical Brownian motion, which reflects how snowflakes form in the real world. (2021)
Gravity Wells — Thousands of points leave glowing trails as they float towards a number of gravity wells around the canvas, leaving flowing patterns in their wake. (2022)

— Cellular Automata —

Cellular automata are simulations where cells are arranged in a grid or line and have simple rules applied to them over time, so that each cell changes state as a function of time


Conway's Game of Life — A customizable 2D cellular automaton simulation based in simple rules that creates complex and chaotic results. (2021)
Elementary Cellular Automaton — A simple, two-state 1D cellular automaton simulation with 256 selectable rules. (2021)
Ternary Elementary Cellular Automaton — A colorful, complex, three-state 1D cellular automaton simulation with over 7 trillion selectable rules. (2021)
Totalistic Cellular Automaton — A four-state 1D cellular automaton with a over 1 million simple rules that result in interesting outputs. (2021)
Block Cellular Automaton — A basic two-state, 1D block-neighborhood cellular automaton with 256 selectable rules. (2021)
Continuous Cellular Automaton — A cellular automaton where each cell can range from 0% to 100% brightness, creating complex gradient patterns. (2021)
Langton's Ant — A customizable 2D cellular automaton simulation involving simple ants. (work in progress) (2021)
